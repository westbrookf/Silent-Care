personal care services

We assist individuals with physical disabilities, mental impairments, and other health care needs with their activities of daily living (ADLs).

Our assistive personnel also provide bedside care—including basic nursing procedures—all under the supervision of a registered nurse, licensed practical nurse or other health care professional. APs must demonstrate their abilities and competencies

before gaining any expanded responsibilities within the clinical setting. While providing this care, UAPs offer

"letting our hands work for you gives you the break you deserve."

compassion and patience and are part of the patient's healthcare support system. Communication is key between our APs and Registered Nurses (RN) as they work together in best interest of the patient.

We provide care for patients in hospitals, residents of nursing facilities, clients in private homes, and others in need of our services.

We provide private duty service to individuals who would like to self-pay.

We provide In-Home services through Mo Medicaid services.

Our care givers have been trained to meet your medical needs.

We have quality trained care givers for your asl needs.
